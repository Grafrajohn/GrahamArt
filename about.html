Graham was born in Scotland. He has lived in Lincolnshire, Derbyshire and Yorkshire in northern England. He currently lives in Yorkshire, England.

Graham was educated at the University of Leeds, England and Heriot Watt University, Edinburgh, Scotland. He works in the commercial digital industries in the area of design and implementation. He has travelled to Denmark, France, Germany, Holland, Belgium, Italy, Spain, Portugal, Greece, Malta, Norway, Scotland, Ireland, Czech Republic, Sweden, Finland, Poland, Russia, Turkey, Morocco, Tunisia, Australia, Singapore, India, Mexico, United States of America and Canada.

Graham is a member of East Riding Artists and participates in exhibitions.

Graham can sometimes be seen playing amateurish guitar and singing poorly in folk venues in North Yorkshire.

Materials and techniques

The oil paintings featured in these web pages were painted with Windsor and Newton Artists, Talens Rembrandt or Sennelier Extra-Fine Artists’ quality oil paints on stretched primed canvases, with some acrylic underpainting. Watercolours are painted in Winsor and Newton Artists watercolours, gouache and artists’ ink on heavy acid-free watercolour paper.
