Graham was born in Scotland. He has lived in Lincolnshire, Derbyshire and Yorkshire in northern England. He currently lives in Yorkshire, England.

Graham was educated at the University of Leeds, England and Heriot Watt University, Edinburgh, Scotland. He works in the commercial digital industries in the area of design and implementation. He has travelled to Denmark, France, Germany, Switzerland, Holland, Belgium, Italy, Spain, Portugal, Greece, Malta, Norway, Scotland, Wales, Ireland, Czech Republic, Sweden, Finland, Poland, Russia, Turkey, Morocco, Tunisia, Australia, Singapore, India, Mexico, Colombia, Peru, United States of America and Canada.

Graham's artistic influences include Gauguin, Vermeer, Van Dyck, Van Gogh, Rousseau, Hopper, Basquiat and many more.

The works

The oil paintings featured in these web pages were painted with Windsor and Newton Artists, Talens Rembrandt or Sennelier Extra-Fine Artists’ quality oil paints on stretched primed canvases, with some acrylic underpainting. Watercolours are painted in Winsor and Newton Artists watercolours, gouache and artists’ ink on heavy acid-free watercolour paper.

Graham's artistic statement

I believe that art of all kinds is food for the soul.

My aim is to make art which follows the ABC of art:

  • Accessible
  • Beautiful
  • Contentful

As I and my work develop and change I will continue to paint and see where the journey takes me.
