It is one of my favourite places. artistnote1: Again here I was attracted to the geometric shapes and patterns in the structure of the hall. Some of the patterns are functional, others decorative. The overall effect is one of great beauty. artistnote2: Houses of this type and era were about expression of the status of the inhabitants. Architcture as art. There is also something English about the dour black and white colours of the building. I tried to capture the feeling that the building stands tall and proud in our modern world, a gift from the age that built it, proud and noble. artistnote3: The hall is now in the care of the National Trust and guided tours are available. link-info: 'https://www.nationaltrust.org.uk/little-moreton-hall' permalink: little-moreton ---
