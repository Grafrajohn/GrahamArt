Welcome to my online fine art gallery

My online art gallery has been on the Internet since 1996 and has won several awards. I hope you enjoy my original paintings and prints.

I became interested in painting after reading books about the techniques of the great masters, which my father owned. Particularly a book about the post-impressionists piqued my interest due to the vibrant colours and interesting composition of this group of artists.
